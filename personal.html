My Experience

Why Do I Love This Memory?

I love this memory because it's just so special. Eating it after I hadn't for an extremely long time, paired with the fact that it had been grown in my house just made me feel even better eating it.

Where Did It Happen?

This happened in my house.

Why Is This A Lasting Memory?

This is a lasting memory partially because it happened recently and because it stuck out among my other memories because of how much it meant to me.

  2. happy because I had actually watched the fruit grow.
